Frequently Asked

Most of our pieces are intentionally designed with a boxy, oversized fit. If you prefer a regular fit, we recommend sizing down. Please check our detailed Sizing Guide for exact measurements before purchasing.

Yes, we ship worldwide. International shipping usually takes 5-10 business days depending on customs. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout via WhatsApp based on your exact location.

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging with all tags attached. Limited drop items are final sale.

To maintain the premium quality and heavy weight of our cotton, we strictly recommend machine washing cold inside out, and hanging to dry. Do not tumble dry, as this can cause shrinkage and damage to high-density prints.
